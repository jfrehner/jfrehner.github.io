Hi, I'm Jonas Frehner,
a curious Fullstack-Software Engineer

I love solving problems, fixing weird bugs in long-forgotten places in enterprise applications, and helping to construct visually pleasing and easy-to-use UIs. This site shall be container for all the stuff I learn or find interesting and which I do not want to forget (and for which I find the time to write a post :D ).

A little bit about myself: On the frontend-side I work with HTML, CSS and Javascript. I have extensive working experience using Angular 2+, Redux (ngrx, rxjs), HTML, SCSS and structuring enterprise sized frontend applications. In recent projects I gained a solid understanding and experience in implementing accessible websites, the WCAG-standard and also helped introducing automated accessibility-tests. I have also a bit of know-how regarding usability and graphic design (but by no means I would regard myself an expert in it).

On the backend-side I usually work with Java (plain old Java, but also Spring and SpringBoot) - though I have also realized projects in node.js and PHP.

I hope you'll find the articles I'll post here useful and if not - there's always stackoverflow to the rescue.
